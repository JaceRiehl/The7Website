Homelander

Homelander, the leader of The 7 is the most powerful Supe in the world. He leads a heroic team of Supes that live their lives protecting America.

Queen Maeve

With incredible superhuman abilities, Queen Maeve is an incredible asset to The 7. She has Incredible strength, speed, and durability that allow her to accompany Homelander on any mission he has. As well, Queen Maeve is the only LGBT member of The 7. She is a idol for anyone to look up to as she fights for equality.

Starlight

The newest member of The 7, Starlight, was gods gift to Vought. Starlight was born and raised as a Christian and she still is today. Starlight has a passion for fighting crime, before joining The 7 she was saving people on the streets on New York. Starlight's main power is the ability to manipulate electricity through flashes of light in her eyes and palms.

A-Train

A-Train is the fastest man alive. He has speed beyond the human eye. Born with this gift of speed he knew that he needed to do what was right to fight crime. Thus, he joined The 7 to continue his goal of making New York City the safest place on earth.

Black Noir

Black Noir is the best hand to hand fighter in The 7. He based his costume on contemporary ninjas. He's stealthy, highly skilled, and incredibly powerful.

Translucent

Translucent is as his name describes. He's the only man capable of becoming completely invisible. This gives The 7 an edge on crime, never allowing criminals to see what's coming.

The Deep

The Deep has an incredible ability. He is able to communicate and team with aquatic life. This has given the world incite it had never had before into the mind of some of the worlds smartest animals. His ability also gives us to not only keep the land and skies safe, but the sea as well.
